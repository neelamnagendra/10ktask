Throwback: Chief Justice Chandrachud on his vision for Indian judiciary at Conclave

Chief Justice ofIndia Dhananjay Yashwant Chandrachud completed one year in office on Thursday.
Over the past year, the 63-year-old judge has demonstrated unwavering commitment to fundamental principles such as liberty, free speech,women's rights, and LGBTQIA+ inclusion, while also striving to maintain a delicate balance among the three branches of the State. A tech-savvy leader with a liberal bent, Chief Justice Chandrachud assumed office with high expectations.
Known for his outspoken nature, he has delivered verdicts on pivotal issues impacting public life and initiated reforms aimed at enhancing the efficiency of judicial system in the years ahead.
